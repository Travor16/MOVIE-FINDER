Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Movie Finders. We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining transparent data practices. This policy outlines how we handle data within our platform.

1. Information We Process

Our platform uses advanced analysis to recognize media elements from user-provided images or video frames. Here is how that data is managed:

  • User Uploads: Media frames or screenshots uploaded by you are processed temporarily to perform title identification via our serverless edge functions. These files are not stored, sold, or retained permanently on our servers.
  • Anonymized Queries: Search queries or extracted titles are passed securely to third-party metadata providers (such as TMDB and Watchmode) to fetch relevant movie details and regional availability details. No identifying personal information is appended to these requests.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Links

We may integrate analytics or standard diagnostic tracking utilities to improve service layout performance. Additionally, our platform features external destination links pointing to verified digital distribution networks (e.g., IMDb, Netflix, Prime Video). We hold no administrative authority or liability over the data retention architectures of outside domains.

3. Data Protection and Compliance

We utilize standard industry practices and encryption pathways to secure incoming API data transactions. Because we do not store personalized registration records or authentication profiles, we maintain a baseline structure that isolates personal user details from exposure risk.

4. Contact Us

For explicit inquiries regarding deployment mechanisms or structural questions about this platform, reach out via the communication utilities available on our main home page interface.
