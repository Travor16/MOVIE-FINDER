About Movie Finder UG

Last Updated: July 2026

Movie Finder UG is a web-based platform that allows users to identify movies and TV series from screenshots or video clips using advanced AI-powered visual recognition. Our mission is to make discovering entertainment effortless, whether you saw a clipped scene on social media, a memorable moment in a trailer, or a random frame while browsing.

Our Vision

To become the go-to tool for instant scene-to-screen identification, bridging the gap between fleeting visual memories and the full cinematic experience.

How It Works

  1. Upload a screenshot or short video clip (or type the title if you already know it).
  2. Our AI analyses the visual elements—faces, costumes, props, setting, on-screen text—and matches them against a vast database of movies and TV series.
  3. Within seconds, you receive the exact title, release year, genre, runtime, official trailer, and where to watch it legally across platforms like Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, Showmax, and more.
  4. For users in Uganda and other regions, we also highlight VJ‑dubbed versions where available.

Data & Privacy

We respect your privacy. Uploaded media is processed temporarily and never stored permanently. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.
